Support L

GOLD CERTIFIED LEATHER
for a sustainable future.

As we research and incorporate best green practices, we are working with suppliers who are on the forefront of responsible manufacturing. We insist that all of the leather we use in our products is sourced from gold-rated tanneries that use less water, less energy, and eliminate wastewater pollution.

SUSTAINING FORESTS
FOR GENERATIONS TO COME.

Yulex was making wetsuits out of natural rubber, sustainably harvested in Forest Stewardship Council-approved regions. We discovered that the more natural rubber we used in our boots, the better they performed. BOGS Seamless Yulex boots are lighter and more durable than other boots. Classic win-win.

Nature is the best
Disinfectant.

As counter-intuitive as it sounds, some odor protection technologies have pesticides in them. We discovered that natural probiotics do an equally effective job without polluting our waterways with harmful chemicals.

BY LIMITING WASTE WE
WE EXPAND THE POSSIBILITIES.

Our rubber factories minimize waste by using as much as 40% post-industrial recycled material. You’ll find recycled laces, outsoles, webbing, textiles and binding in our Classic Casual line.

CLEANING LAKES & RIVERS
EVERY STEP OF THE WAY future.

BOGS is partnering with BLOOM to integrate algae-based EVA footbeds across our entire line. These replace the petroleum-based plastic foam found in most boots and athletic shoes. BLOOM has their own mission, which we’re excited to be a part of: cleaner air and lakes, rivers, and streams.
