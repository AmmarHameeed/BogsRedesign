Our REUSE A BOOT program keeps our products out of landfill and supports some amazing organizations.

When youre done with your BOGS, send them to us. (Well pay for shipping!) If they still have love to give, well clean them up and send them to an outdoor education non-profit. If theyve reached their end of life, well work with our partner, Recircled, to recycle your old boots into something new.

Thanks for helping us reduce our bootprint.
